## Shop and Economy

### Vendor System

**Purpose:** Purchase and sell items to manage your inventory and economy

**Access:**
- Via Main Game Menu
- Shop button in navigation
- May appear in story as location

**Vendor Types:**
- **General Store:** Basic items, weapons, armor, potions
- **Weapon Shop:** Specialized weapons
- **Armor Shop:** Specialized armor
- **Special Vendors:** Unique items, rare equipment

### Buying Items

**Shop Interface:**
- **Buy Tab:** Shows available items for purchase
- Item cards display:
  - Item name and icon
  - Item type badge
  - Price in coins
  - Stat bonuses (if applicable)
  - Level requirement (if any)
  - Stock count (if limited)

**Purchase Process:**
1. Navigate to Shop page
2. Select "Buy" tab
3. Browse available items
4. Click "Buy" button on desired item
5. Confirm purchase (if required)
6. Item added to inventory
7. Coins deducted

**Purchase Requirements:**
- Sufficient coins
- Meet level requirement (if any)
- Item in stock (if limited)
- Inventory space available

**Reputation Discounts:**
- Higher vendor reputation = better prices
- Maximum discount: 20%
- Earned through repeated purchases
- Shown as reduced price

### Selling Items

**Shop Interface:**
- **Sell Tab:** Shows your inventory items
- Item cards display:
  - Item name and icon
  - Sell price (usually 50% of buy price)
  - Current quantity

**Selling Process:**
1. Navigate to Shop page
2. Select "Sell" tab
3. Browse your inventory
4. Click "Sell" button on item
5. Confirm sale (if required)
6. Item removed from inventory
7. Coins added

**Sell Price:**
- Typically 50% of purchase price
- Varies by item rarity
- Legendary items sell for more

### Coin System

**Currency:** Coins (ðŸª™)

**Earning Coins:**
- Completing quests
- Defeating enemies
- Selling items
- Finding treasure
- Completing achievements

**Spending Coins:**
- Buying items from shops
- Crafting materials
- Upgrading equipment
- Special services

**Coin Display:**
- Shown in top HUD
- Yellow badge with coin icon
- Format: "ðŸª™ [amount]"
- Updates in real-time

### Reputation System

**How It Works:**
- Earn reputation by trading with vendors
- Each purchase increases reputation
- Higher reputation = better discounts
- Maximum discount: 20%

**Reputation Display:**
- Shown in vendor interface
- Progress bar or level indicator
- Affects prices automatically

**Benefits:**
- Lower prices on purchases
- Access to special items (at high reputation)
- Better sell prices (potential)

### Discounts

**Reputation-Based Discounts:**
- Calculated automatically
- Applied at checkout
- Maximum: 20% off
- Shown as reduced price

**Example:**
- Item price: 100 coins
- Reputation discount: 15%
- Final price: 85 coins
